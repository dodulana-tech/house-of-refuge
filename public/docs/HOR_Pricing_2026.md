# The 1M deposit

The admission deposit is **NGN 1,000,000**, split as follows:

### NGN 400,000 — Clinical assessment fee
Covers psychiatrist consultation, diagnostics and clinical workup, multidisciplinary case review, and a written admission decision (with referral if declined). **Consumed once the psychiatrist begins the file.**

### NGN 600,000 — Held toward Month 1
Reserves the family's place on the active waitlist and is applied directly to Month 1 fees on admission. **Held — never consumed unless admission proceeds.** Refundable per the policy below.

> The previously published 150k clinical assessment fee and 50k processing fee are abolished and folded into the 400k. Families now see one number, one breakdown.

# Refund policy

We refund every Naira not yet earned. Published in full — not in small print.

| If… | Then… |
|---|---|
| Within 48 hours of paying the deposit, family changes mind | Full **NGN 1,000,000** refunded |
| Family withdraws between 48hr and the psychiatrist consult | **600k refunded**; 400k retained for clinical work |
| HOR psychiatrist clinically declines admission | **600k refunded** + written referral; 400k retained |
| Family withdraws more than 14 days before admission day | **600k refunded** |
| Family withdraws within 14 days of admission day | 50% of 600k refunded; 50% retained as bed-hold cancellation |
| HOR cannot offer a bed within active waitlist hold | **600k refunded**; 400k retained |
| Family withdraws voluntarily during waitlist hold | **600k refunded** in full, anytime |
| Family declines a slot offer once made | **600k refunded**; place released, no haggling |
| Within 72 hours of admission, resident withdraws | **80%** of total Month 1 paid refunded; 400k not refundable |
| Withdrawal after 72 hours of admission | No refund (no exceptions) |
| Medical discharge after Month 1 (clinical reasons) | Months 2+ refunded pro-rata |

# Active waitlist mechanics

- **Active hold = 6 months**, renewable on family confirmation if they wish to keep waiting.
- **Monthly check-in.** Admissions team contacts the family each month to confirm continued interest. **Two missed contacts** = automatic waitlist removal + 600k refund.
- **Voluntary withdrawal** at any time during the hold = 600k refunded in full.
- **Single declined slot offer** = removal from waitlist + 600k refund. No haggling.
- **Re-assessment after 6 months** = NGN 100,000 (lighter cost on a known clinical case).
